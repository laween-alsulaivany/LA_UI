Privacy

Effective May 2026

No data collected

LA UI is a static design system reference guide. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no analytics trackers, cookies, session tokens, or server-side processing of any kind.

No accounts or forms

No account is required to use this guide. No form submissions are stored. All pages are static HTML served locally, so nothing leaves your machine.

Third-party links

Some links in this guide point to external sites such as GitHub. Those sites have their own privacy policies. LA UI does not control their practices.

Questions

If you have a question about this policy, open an issue on GitHub.
