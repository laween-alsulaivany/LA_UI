Privacy

Effective May 2026

No data collected

LA UI is a static design system reference guide. The guide itself does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. It does not include analytics trackers, cookies, accounts, session tokens, or server-side processing.

No accounts or forms

No account is required to use this guide. The included pages do not submit forms or store form data. When viewed locally, the guide runs from files on your machine.

Third-party links

Some links in this guide point to external sites such as GitHub. If the guide is hosted publicly, the hosting provider may receive normal request data. External sites have their own privacy practices.
